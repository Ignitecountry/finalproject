About The Event

Utah's First-Ever Country Dance Convention

Enter the arena. Utah Dance Fest is Utah's first ever country swing convention that started in September of 2024. It is a premier country swing and line dance convention — four days of non-stop dancing, learning, competing, and connecting with the best in the region.

With Country Swing and Line Dance as our main events, we are now adding West Coast Swing, Progressive, Slotted, and Zouk — if you love dancing and learning, this is your event. Come ready to sweat, laugh, learn from world-class instructors, and make memories that last all year.

This year's theme? The Hunger Games. Districts named. Champions crowned. The dance floor is your arena!

Inside The Arena

Partner Dance Workshops

Country Swing, Progressive, West Coast Swing, and more — taught by nationally recognized instructors.

Line Dance Workshops

Beginner to advanced line dance choreography. Learn new dances and master the classics across multiple levels and styles.

Competitions

Partner or line dance divisions. All skill levels from newcomer through champion. GA pass required to compete.

Nightly Social Dances

After workshops and competitions, the real magic begins. Good music, late-night socials, and hours of dancing with your fellow tributes.

Artist Showcases & Demos

Community & Connection

Find your district and become part of Utah's fastest-growing country dance community. Create a network of dancers across the nation and spread the love of dancing. This is your tribe.

The visionary behind Utah's first-ever country dance convention. Franz has dedicated years to building a country dance community in Utah, founding Ignite Country Fusion and dreaming up an event that brings dancers of all levels together under one roof — and one incredible theme.
